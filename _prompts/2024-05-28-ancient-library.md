DALL-E 3's strength here is **painterly coherence** — the Piranesi/Vermeer reference created a consistent light logic that Midjourney tends to break with HDR over-processing.

The word "living mountain" (rather than "cave" or "cavern") shifted the material palette dramatically: the model read "living" as organic, introducing the moss and subtle root structures that made the final image feel inhabited rather than constructed.

### Prompt engineering notes

- "Spiralling upward into mist" prevents the model from closing the ceiling
- "Single oculus" forces the composition to have one dominant light source, improving depth
- Naming two specific artists is more precise than style adjectives like "painterly" alone
